About Marcus Bohm & the Bang!!

Listen to our debut album "56" on Spotify now!
Also avaliable on vinyl at Åmål Records.

Marcus Bohm - vocals & guitar
Dee Castro - Bass
Maria Doumeni - keyboards, cello & backing vocals
Tobias Kjerstaduis - guitars & backing vocals
Isac Cronholm - drums & percussion

The music we play is melodic pop with roots in the 80's and with branches into the future. We sing in swedish but the music is universal so everyone from Paris, to China to Colorado is a potential target.
We formed in the fall of 2016 and released our debut album in April 2017, which was basically recorded by Marcus and the producer Love Tholin, before the band took shape. Love is also the owner of our record company, Åmål Records.
